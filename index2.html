ROHIT SHARMA


Rohit Sharma, often called the Hitman of Indian cricket, is one of the most elegant and explosive batsmen of the modern era. Known for his effortless timing, calm temperament, and ability to score big hundreds, he has etched his name in cricket history with multiple double centuries in ODIs — a feat unmatched by any other player. As India’s captain, Rohit is admired for his sharp cricketing mind, strategic decisions, and ability to lead with composure under pressure. Whether dominating bowlers with graceful shots or guiding his team with confidence, Rohit Sharma continues to be an inspiration for millions of cricket fans around the world.
