virat kohli


Virat Kohli is one of the most accomplished and influential cricketers of the modern era, known for his exceptional batting skill, unwavering determination, and remarkable consistency across all formats of the game. Born on 5 November 1988, he rose to prominence with his aggressive playing style and strong leadership qualities. As a former captain of the Indian cricket team, Kohli led India to significant victories, including a historic Test series win in Australia. His discipline, fitness, and passion for the sport have made him a role model for aspiring cricketers around the world. With numerous records to his name, Virat Kohli continues to be celebrated as one of the greatest batsmen in cricket history.
